关于我

大家好，我的呼号是 BA8AKA，来自四川成都。2024 年 7 月，我顺利通过了业余无线电操作证书考试，并在 8 月正式获得了个人呼号和电台执照。那时，我只是因为一次偶然的机会接触到了业余无线电，但没想到从此便走进了一个充满乐趣和挑战的世界。无线电不仅是一种通信工具，更像是一扇窗口，让我与世界产生了全新的联系。 目前我使用的设备包括泉盛 UV-K6 手持台、如意通 6900 DMR 移动台，以及一台 MMDVM 数字热点盒子，天线则是 1.2 米玻璃钢天线。随着这些设备逐渐投入使用，我开始在 VHF/UHF 频段上进行模拟和数字通联，也尝试通过 DMR 网络与世界各地的电台交流。每一次成功的通联，不论是近距离的本地联络，还是跨越网络与远方的呼叫，都会让我感受到无线电带来的独特魅力。 我对业余无线电的兴趣主要集中在几个方向：一是数字语音与网络互通，通过 MMDVM 热点探索 DMR、Fusion、D-STAR 等不同制式的应用；二是天线设计与优化，我会尝试不同的架设方式，比较信号覆盖和稳定性的差异；三是业余无线电在应急通信中的潜在应用，思考在突发情况下如何借助无线电提供有效的通信支持。 创建这个网站的初衷，就是想把我的学习过程和实践经历记录下来，不仅作为个人的成长笔记，也希望能为同样热爱无线电的朋友提供一些参考。在这里，我会分享心得体会、技术探索和资源资料，更期待与大家展开交流。 如果你和我一样热爱业余无线电，或者对我的分享内容有兴趣，欢迎随时联系我。我的邮箱是 ba8aka@qq.com 。希望这个小小的站点，能成为我们共同交流与成长的地方。
Hello everyone, my callsign is BA8AKA, and I am based in Chengdu, Sichuan, China. In July 2024, I passed the amateur radio license examination, and by August I was officially assigned my callsign and radio license. What started as a chance encounter with amateur radio quickly turned into a passion, opening the door to a fascinating world full of discovery, challenges, and endless possibilities. My current station setup includes a Quansheng UV-K6 handheld transceiver, a Ruyitong 6900 DMR mobile radio, an MMDVM hotspot for digital modes, and a 1.2-meter fiberglass antenna. With these, I have been active on the VHF/UHF bands, exploring both analog and digital communications. I also enjoy experimenting with DMR and connecting with operators around the world through digital networks. Each successful QSO—whether it’s a short local contact or a long-distance link via the network—brings me a unique sense of excitement and fulfillment. My main interests in amateur radio lie in digital voice and network interconnectivity, experimenting with DMR, Fusion, and D-STAR through MMDVM; antenna design and optimization, testing different setups to compare coverage and stability; and exploring the role of amateur radio in emergency communications, considering how it can provide reliable links when conventional systems fail. This website, ba8aka.com, is my way of documenting this journey. It serves as both a personal log of my learning and experimentation, and a platform to share knowledge, resources, and insights with fellow radio enthusiasts. If you share a love for amateur radio or find my work interesting, I’d be glad to connect with you. You can reach me at ba8aka@qq.com . I hope this site can become a space where we can learn, exchange, and grow together in the spirit of amateur radio.
